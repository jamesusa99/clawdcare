About BingoHealth

AI in the box, privacy at the edge. BingoHealth puts an embodied agent on your desk or shelf—sensitive health context stays on your M.2 vault unless you opt into cloud.

Inspired by the BingoClaw “local × cloud × open API” story, tuned for US families and clinicians-in-the-loop wellness.

Platform

Credit plans and capabilities

BingoHealth runs on BingoClaw. Accounts combine monthly Programs with a Credit Plan that adds credits each billing cycle for on-demand AI and automations. Credit packs are one-time top-ups when you need more before the next cycle, alongside optional hardware on this site.

Mission

Longer healthspan, on your terms

“Not only treat disease—slow decline and give everyone more years of healthy life, with hardware and AI you control.”
