Overview

How ClawdCare billing fits together—same ideas as a typical console Settings → Billing area.

Programs

Monthly guided subscriptions from the Shop. Coaching and tracks—not metered per credit.

Credit plan & packs

Monthly Credit Plan refills your balance each cycle. Mid-cycle top-up: Credit packs (one-time) in the Shop. Manage your plan under Subscriptions.

Payment

Saved cards pay for hardware and recurring charges. Set a default for renewals under Payment.

Orders

Hardware bundles and one-time services appear under Orders when commerce APIs are connected.

Subscriptions

Programs are monthly guided subscriptions from the Shop—coaching, tracks, and longevity workflows (not per-credit metering). Each active line bills until you cancel it.

Credit Plan is billed monthly like Programs—typically one active plan line per account. Your balance is summarized in the card above. Need more credits before the next cycle? Use Credit packs (one-time), not a second Credit Plan. Use Edit / Cancel the same way as Programs.

Programs

Monthly Programs from Shop → Programs. Edit the display label or next renewal date here; Cancel stops future charges (effective per your payment processor once billing is connected).
