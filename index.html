Why ClawdCare

Positioning

Three ideas define the product—clear, defensible, and built into the stack.

01 · TRUST

Privacy is the product

Genomics and imaging do not belong in a default cloud bucket. ClawdCare anchors trust with on-device storage and explicit paths when you choose cloud compute.

02 · DEPTH

Continuity beats visits

Episodic care leaves gaps—memory, personalization, aging, reminders. ClawdCare is the always-on layer that holds your timeline and closes the loop between data and action.

03 · ACCESS

Zero DevOps health AI

Scan, sign in, go. No model menus or YAML—BingoClaw surfaces capabilities through ClawdCare so households get serious AI without becoming sysadmins.
