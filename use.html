Product use · Personal health center

Your personal health center—powered by ClawdCare.

After you order ClawdCare, the platform is designed to become the place where you continuously add health information—vitals, labs, imaging reports, medications, and daily notes. ClawdCare helps you organize, connect, and act on that story over time, with a strong bias toward healthy aging and prevention, while keeping sensitive data under your roof by default.

How it works

Three loops you run for life.

1

Capture anytime

Speak to the device, scan a PDF, pull in wearable summaries, or type a quick note—so your record isn’t trapped in “last visit only.”

2

Structure & memory

ClawdCare turns scattered inputs into a longitudinal timeline: trends, context, and reminders—so small shifts don’t get lost.

3

Act with judgment

Programs, nudges, and (where appropriate) care-team handoffs are designed to support wellness and adherence—not replace your clinician.

Where you manage it

Console + account = your operating model.

Console

Pair hardware, watch imports, tune skills and automations, and review alerts—your day-to-day control surface for ClawdCare.

My account

Identity, orders, preferences, and (over time) how your health profile syncs with the device—your user management home on the web.
