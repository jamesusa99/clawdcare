Shop

ClawdCare products fall into three categories: Hardware (typically one-time purchase), Programs (monthly guided services), and Token Plan (monthly subscription that credits your token balance for on-demand AI, skills, and automations). You can buy more than one Token Plan—if you use up this month’s tokens early, add another Token Plan from the Shop to top up (each active plan bills monthly on its own line). When a Program and one or more Token Plans are active, those charges recur every month until you change or cancel. Add any mix to your cart, adjust quantities below, then Place Order to start—we confirm total, tax, shipping, and payment.

Token Plan

Monthly token subscription

Token Plan subscriptions increase your token balance each billing cycle. Tokens are consumed when you run on-demand AI, console skills, automations, and cloud assists—separate from monthly Programs. You do not pick raw model SKUs. You can purchase Token Plans multiple times (e.g. raise quantity in the cart or check out again): if this month’s allocation runs out before the next cycle, buy another Token Plan to credit more tokens. Each active plan is $/month until you cancel that line. Add tiers with hardware or programs in one order when you like.
