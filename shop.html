ClawdCare commerce has four product types: Hardware (typically one-time), Programs (monthly guided services), Credit Plan (monthly subscription that credits your credit balance each cycle), and Credit packs (one-time purchases that add credits immediately—your top-up when you need more before the next plan cycle). The first three bill on a monthly rhythm where noted; credit packs are pay once per pack. Add items to your cart, adjust quantities where allowed, then Place Order to start—we confirm total, tax, shipping, and payment.

Credit Plan

Monthly Credit Subscription

Credit Plan is a monthly subscription: each cycle adds to your credit balance for on-demand AI, console skills, automations, and cloud assists—separate from Programs. Your cart holds one Credit Plan tier at a time—picking a different tier replaces the previous. If you run through credits before the next renewal, use Credit packs (below) for a one-time top-up—not an extra Credit Plan line.
